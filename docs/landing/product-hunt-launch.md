2. Schedule launch for Tuesday–Thursday, 00:01 AM PST.
3. Prepare assets:

   - Screenshots: Grafana dashboard, Swagger UI, Helm deploy
   - First comment: introduce yourself and the project
4. Share on social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit).
